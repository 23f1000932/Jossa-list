Understanding the Data

NIT Quota Structure: Each NIT reserves 50% seats for Home State (HS) candidates (Class 12 from that state) and 50% for Other State (OS). HS closing ranks are usually significantly more relaxed. For example, NIT Trichy CSE OS general closed at ~1,449 in 2025, while HS closed at ~4,500. For a student from UP (home state of MNNIT Allahabad, NIT Jamshedpur vicinity), HS quota can be a major advantage.
EWS Category Important: The EWS rank used in cutoffs is your JEE Main EWS category rank, NOT your CRL. A student with CRL 2,00,000 may have EWS rank ~30,000. EWS cutoffs at NITs/IIITs are noticeably more relaxed than general — roughly equivalent to OBC-NCL cutoffs or slightly tighter. At Tier-3/4 NITs, EWS rank 30,000 gives access to CSE.
CSAB Special Rounds: After JoSAA Round 6, CSAB special rounds open up vacant seats (mainly in NITs, IIITs, GFTIs). CSAB cutoffs are typically 5–15% looser than JoSAA R6. This is a second opportunity — register separately at csab.nic.in after JoSAA.

Key Strategic Rules for Your Situation

Rule 1 — Always prioritize EWS over General: For every institute, fill EWS category seats before General seats of the same branch at the same institute. Your EWS rank is far stronger than your CRL.
Rule 2 — Use Float/Slide aggressively: Select Float in early rounds. This keeps you in the running for upgrades while holding your current seat. Never select Freeze unless you are completely satisfied with the allotted seat.
Rule 3 — Fill 80–100 choices without penalty: JoSAA has no penalty for filling more choices. Fill all realistic options including T4 NITs, T3/T4 IIITs, and GFTIs as backups. A seat at NIT Mizoram under EWS is better than no seat.
Rule 4 — Consider CSAB carefully: If JoSAA gives you only a very weak option (GFTI or T4 NIT-General), don't accept and wait — participate in CSAB. But if you get NIT Manipur/Meghalaya/Sikkim under EWS, that's valuable — consider accepting.
Rule 5 — Placement truth: At T4 NITs and T3/T4 IIITs, your personal skills, competitive programming, internships, and CGPA matter far more than the college brand. A motivated student from NIT Manipur can still land a ₹15–20 LPA job with strong DSA and projects. Don't get demoralized by tier labels.
