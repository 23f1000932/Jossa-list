⚠ EWS Ranks Are Category-Specific
EWS cutoffs in JoSAA are EWS category ranks — a completely separate rank list from CRL. EWS rank of 30,000 is verified against josaa.nic.in EWS column, not CRL. This distinction is critical — see Overview tab for full explanation.
Overview & Critical Notes on Data
Read this tab first — it explains how to correctly interpret all ranks in this report.
CRITICAL — EWS Ranks Explained: In JoSAA, the EWS closing rank shown in the OR-CR tool is the EWS category rank, not the CRL. EWS candidates have their own rank list generated from their JEE Main performance among all EWS-declared candidates. As a result, EWS closing ranks at top NITs appear very low numerically (e.g., NIT Trichy EWS CSE = 257, NIT Warangal = 309). This does NOT mean EWS is easier to crack — it means the 257th-ranked person among all EWS candidates nationwide got a seat there. An EWS rank of 30,000 means you are the 30,000th-ranked among all EWS-eligible JEE Main students. Source: josaa.nic.in official OR-CR data + cracku.in verified table (June 2026).
